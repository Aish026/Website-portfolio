FAQ

Common Questions

Do I own the website after you build it? +

Yes, 100%. Once you pay the balance, the website is fully yours. I hand over all files and login details. You're not locked into anything with me.

What if I don't like the design? +

Every package includes 2 free revision rounds. I share a preview before going live — you don't pay the balance until you're satisfied with the result.

How does payment work? +

50% advance to start, 50% only after you've seen and approved the website. UPI / bank transfer. No surprises.

What do I need to provide? +

Just your logo (if you have one), photos of your shop/products, your services list, address and phone number. No logo? No problem — I can work with what you have.

Will I be able to update the website myself? +

For small changes (text, prices, photos) I offer maintenance at ₹500/month. Or you can WhatsApp me any time for a quick quote on updates.

I'm based outside Kharghar — can you still build my website? +

Absolutely. Everything is done over WhatsApp — I work with clients anywhere in India. Location doesn't matter.
